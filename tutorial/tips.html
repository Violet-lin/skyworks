Common tips and examples

While exporting images for your development,
you realised that some of the files were not as straightforward as you imagine.
  • Now TV tickets. Remove clipping mask.
    Place your mouse in between the TV show and ticket mask.
    Hold Alt and click between the two layers to remove/add clipping Mask.

    The "holes" of the tickets are removed.
  • Find x & y position for images. (For DCO and Bespoke Banner)
    To get correct x and y position for DCO banners (Headline, CTA and images position).
    cmd+T

    It shows detail infomation of x, y, width, height and degree infomation of the item you selected.
    You can select reference point by clicking
    "top-left", "top-center" or "middle-center"

    Reference point is equivalent to regX and regY in CreateJS.

    Draw an area that is the same size of the sentence,

    cmd+T to get the exact value of reference point (regX/regY).
